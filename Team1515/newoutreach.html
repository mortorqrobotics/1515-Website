Albion Street

This year we visited Albion Street Elementary for the second time. We demonstrated how the robot operates and students had the opportunity to participate in a game of catch with it. We look forward to another fun-filled visit next year!

Women In STEM

As a high-school robotics team we bring STEM to our communities and enable students to grow by hosting events such as the Women in STEM Speaker Series, where female professionals talk about their successes and challenges. The event is normally hosted at our district middle school to maximize participation. Instead, we hosted a Women in STEM event via Zoomwhich was posted on our school's media class's YouTube channel. We recently hosted our second virtual Women in STEM event and are currently planning our next one.

Friendship Circle

We also took our robot to the Friendship Circle, an organization that provides programming for children with special needs. We showed the children our robot, how we control it, and also did a robot related arts and crafts activity with them.
